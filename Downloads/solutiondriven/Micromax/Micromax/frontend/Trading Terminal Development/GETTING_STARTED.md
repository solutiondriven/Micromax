# Micromax Trading Bot - Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started with Micromax

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. **Open** the Micromax app in your browser
2. **Click** the login area (blurred trading chart)
3. **Choose Sign Up** at the bottom of the modal
4. **Enter:**
   - Email address
   - Password (at least 6 characters)
   - Full name
   - Phone number (optional)
5. **Click Sign Up**
6. **Congratulations!** Your account is created

### Step 2: Sign In

1. **Click** the login area again
2. **Choose Sign In**
3. **Enter** your email and password
4. **Click Sign In**
5. **Dashboard appears!** You're logged in

## Telegram Notifications Setup

Get instant trade alerts directly on Telegram!

### Step 1: Connect with Telegram Bot

1. **In Micromax:**
   - Click your profile icon (top right)
   - Scroll to "Micromax Bot" section
   - Click "Start Bot & Connect"

2. **Telegram opens automatically**
   - Watch for the @Impulsehub_bot
   - Click START button

3. **Get your Telegram ID:**
   - Follow the bot instructions
   - The bot will show you your ID
   - Copy the 10-digit number

### Step 2: Paste Your ID

1. **Back in Micromax:**
   - The input field is ready for your ID
   - Paste the 10-digit number
   - ID must be numeric (no spaces or special characters)

### Step 3: Verify Connection

1. **Click "Save & Send Test Message"**
2. **Check Telegram:**
   - You should receive a test message
   - ✅ Connection successful!
3. **Start trading!**
   - You'll now get notifications

## Troubleshooting

### "Failed to Sign Up"

**Problem:** Email already registered
- Open Sign In instead
- Click "Forgot Password" if needed

**Problem:** Password too short
- Password must be at least 6 characters

**Problem:** Invalid email
- Check email format: user@domain.com

### "Telegram ID Not Connecting"

**Problem:** Wrong format
- Telegram IDs must be numbers only
- No spaces, letters, or special characters
- Should be 7-10 digits long

**Problem:** Still not receiving messages
1. Make sure you've clicked START in @Impulsehub_bot
2. Check your Telegram privacy settings
3. The bot might not be started yet

**Problem:** "Invalid Telegram ID format"
- Copy only the number from the bot
- Paste exactly as shown (no edits)

### "Session Expired"

**Problem:** You got logged out
- Just sign in again
- Your session lasts 1 hour
- Active use auto-extends the session

**Problem:** Lost connection
- Check your internet
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
- Sign in again

### Dark Mode Not Working

**Problem:** App appears in light mode
- Click theme toggle (sun/moon icon)
- Should switch to dark theme
- Preference saved

## Security Tips

### Protect Your Account

1. **Use a strong password**
   - Mix numbers, letters, capitals
   - Avoid common words
   - At least 12 characters recommended

2. **Don't share credentials**
   - Never give anyone your password
   - Don't log in on public WiFi
   - Use HTTPS always

3. **Protect your Telegram ID**
   - Keep it private
   - Don't share on social media
   - Only in Micromax app

4. **Watch for phishing**
   - Always use official link
   - Never click suspicious links
   - Official domain: your-micromax-domain.com

### Session Management

- **Auto Logout:** After 1 hour of inactivity
- **Auto Refresh:** Tokens refresh automatically
- **Multiple Devices:** Each device tracked separately
- **Sign Out Everywhere:** Option to logout all devices
